  <p>Most of our federal and state legislators are elected from districts. Every ten years, state governments redraw district boundaries in a process known as redistricting. PlanScore promotes fairness in the redistricting process. We make it easy for policymakers and advocates to score new district maps and assess whether they're fair or gerrymandered. We also provide access to the most comprehensive historical dataset of partisan gerrymandering ever assembled.</p>
